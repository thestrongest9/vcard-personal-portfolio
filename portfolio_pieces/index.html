About me 1

Developer, primarily specializing in game development. I have extensive experience with Unity and Godot. With Unity in particular I've worked with both 2D and 3D development. All of my Godot development has been done in 2D with Godot 4.0+.

With game development in particular, I've worked a lot with AI pathfinding and movement solutions (A*, Flowfield Pathfinding). Recently I've also been researching procedural generation methods to allow for more interesting levels in games. I also have some experience working with WebGL and shaders.

Other than game development, I also work with AI (OpenAI Gymnasium, Stable Baselines 3).
